
How to ride
Reserve or walk up
Tap the scooter icon in the Uber app and reserve a nearby electric scooter, or simply walk up to the vehicle to get started.

Start riding
Scan the QR code on the handlebars to unlock and go. (Or manually enter the 6-digit vehicle identification number.) We encourage you to wear a helmet.

While you ride
To brake anytime, squeeze the lever down on the left handlebar. To go, press the lever down gently on the right handlebar. Start slow—the scooter has zip.

Park responsibly
Make sure you park in the correct area shown on the map in your app—and outside any city no-parking areas. Don’t block walkways, ramps, or any areas that need to be used by people with accessibility needs. Consult your city government’s website for rules on where scooters may be operated.
